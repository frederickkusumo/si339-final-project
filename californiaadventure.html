Disney California Adventure

The Walt Disney company would like vistors of the Disneyland Resort to spend more time in there than just having a one day trip. This is why they end up opening a park right across Disneyland Park and named it as Disney California Adventure. It was heavily California theme as they wanted visitors to be able to visit all corners of California by just visiting the park. However, a lot of visitors started giving bad reviews as the park was not up towards their expectation. Now, the park has changed a lot highlighting popular rides such as the Guardians of the Galaxy-Mission: Mission BREAKOUT! and Radiator Springs Racer rides.
