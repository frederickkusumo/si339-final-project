A lot of the Disneyland parks around the world have the same ride system and theme, but the Disneyland Park located in Anaheim, CA started it all. Though the park has undergone through a lot of changes, remaining many open day rides still stand today such as the Disneyland Railroad, Autopia, Peter Pan's Flight, and Jungle Cruise. Other popular rides in the park include, Big Thunder Mountain Railroad, Haunted Mansion, Space Mountain, Pirates of the Caribbean, and Indiana Jones Adventure.
