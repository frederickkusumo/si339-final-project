Disneyland Resort

The first Disneyland designed by Walt Disney and his brother Roy Disney is located in Anaheim, CA, US. It opened in the year of 1955 and immediately became a hotspot for not only California residents, but also visitors from all around the world. The resort includes two theme parks; Disneyland Park and Disney California Adventure, as well as a shopping district named Downtown Disney.
